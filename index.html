About Me

My name is Justin Acosta, I enjoy long walks through the city, staring at books that contain poorly drawn pictures of beaches. Occasionally, I'll listen to music, and wonder what I'm filling my ears with, and realize that it's my own. This soul crushing moment is swiftly followed by the additional conclusion that the poorly drawn beach is a sketch in my diary.
